About HAPsI

Helping Alleviate Poverty in South India since 2002

Our Story

HAPsI was founded to help alleviate poverty in South India through sustainable development projects focused on education, healthcare, and community empowerment.

Our journey began with the Awake Mercy Home, founded in 2002 to provide family-style care for a small number of needy children. Today, the home houses over 50 children from remote tribal areas, providing them with shelter, education, and a supportive community.

The Awake Mercy Home includes a school, gardens, and a well-used playing area, administered by Krishna Moorthi and Elsie. It has received awards for the outstanding quality of its provision.

Our Mission

To improve the lives of children and communities in South India through sustainable, community-driven initiatives that foster education, healthcare, and economic development.

Our Vision

A world where every child in South India has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities to build a better future.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Treating every individual with dignity and respect
  • Integrity: Maintaining transparency and accountability in all operations
  • Sustainability: Creating lasting change through sustainable solutions
  • Empowerment: Enabling communities to become self-sufficient
  • Collaboration: Working with local partners to maximize impact
