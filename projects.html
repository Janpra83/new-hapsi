Our Projects

How we're making a difference in South India

Transforming Lives

HAPsI works on various projects aimed at improving the quality of life for children and communities in South India. Our approach focuses on sustainable development and empowering local communities.

The Awake Mercy Home

The flagship project of HAPsI, the Awake Mercy Home provides shelter, food, education, and care for over 50 children from disadvantaged backgrounds and remote tribal areas.

Founded in 2002, the home has grown from a small facility to a comprehensive care center that includes residential facilities, a school, gardens, and recreational areas.

Impact:

  • Over 50 children currently housed and cared for
  • Hundreds of children supported over the years
  • Many graduates now employed and self-sufficient
  • Award-winning care facility recognized for excellence

Education Programs

Education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty. HAPsI provides educational opportunities through scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure support.

We work with local schools to improve the quality of education and ensure that children from all backgrounds have access to learning opportunities.

Impact:

  • School supplies provided to hundreds of children
  • Scholarships for higher education
  • Teacher training programs
  • Improved school facilities

Healthcare Initiatives

Good health is essential for children to thrive. HAPsI supports healthcare initiatives that provide medical check-ups, treatments, and health education.

We collaborate with local healthcare providers to ensure that children and communities have access to essential medical services.

Impact:

  • Regular health check-ups for children
  • Vaccination programs
  • Nutrition support for malnourished children
  • Health education workshops

Community Development

Sustainable change requires community involvement. HAPsI works with local communities to implement development projects that address their specific needs.

From infrastructure improvements to skills training, our community development initiatives aim to create lasting positive change.

Impact:

  • Skills training for adults
  • Infrastructure improvements
  • Community gardens and sustainable agriculture
  • Water and sanitation projects
